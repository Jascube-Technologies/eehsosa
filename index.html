Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To foster lifelong connections among Ebute Elefun High School alumni, support educational initiatives, and contribute to community development through collaborative efforts and service.

Vision

To be the most impactful alumni association in Nigeria, recognized for excellence in alumni engagement, educational support, and community contribution.

Our History

Tracing the journey of EEHSOSA from inception to present day

Inception

1985

Ebute Elefun High School graduates formed the first alumni association to maintain connections with their alma mater and fellow graduates.

Formal Registration

2019

The association was formally registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) as a non-profit organization.

Welfare & Scholarship Programs

2023, 2024, 2025

EEHSOSA launched its flagship Welfare program, awarding financial support to indigent Member, Students and School.

Digital Platform

2020

EEHSOSA launches its comprehensive digital platform with member profiles, event management, and community engagement features.
